## FAQ

**Q: Do you connect to my accounts?**
```
No. We never connect to your accounts, billing, or APIs. You manually enter your 
current spend. No credentials, no read access, nothing. Your data is private.
```

**Q: How accurate are the recommendations?**
```
Very accurate. Our audit engine uses current published pricing for all 18+ supported 
plans and applies business logic rules (team size thresholds, seat count optimizations). 
We update pricing as vendors change plans.
```

**Q: Is the audit really free?**
```
Yes, completely free. You can run an audit, get your results, and share your report — 
all at no cost. We capture your email optionally to send your report. That's it.
```

**Q: What if I don't know my exact spend?**
```
Just enter your best estimate. Our form shows you the official pricing for each plan 
as a reference, so you can confirm what you're actually paying.
```

**Q: Can I share my report with my team?**
```
Yes. Every audit generates a unique shareable URL like /report/abc123 that shows your 
spending and savings breakdown — with your personal details hidden by default.
```
